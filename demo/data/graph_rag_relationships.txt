Graph RAG in ContextEngine uses the entity_relations table in PostgreSQL instead of a separate
graph database. Demo relations connect ContextEngine to PostgreSQL, pgvector, FlashRank, and
AWS. Additional relations show that pgvector is stored in PostgreSQL and FlashRank reranks
retrieval results.

Relationship questions such as "Which entities are linked to ContextEngine?" route to the
graph retriever. It returns one-hop and optional two-hop evidence as normal source citations
so graph facts participate in verification and answer generation.
